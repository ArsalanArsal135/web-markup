Clubs/Pubs

Wibar in Leiden is a vibrant club celebrated for its energetic atmosphere and eclectic music offerings. It hosts regular events, including themed parties and live DJ performances, appealing to both locals and visitors. The stylish interior, complete with state-of-the-art sound and lighting, creates an immersive experience for dancing and socializing. With a welcoming staff and a commitment to promoting local talent, Wibar fosters a strong sense of community within the nightlife scene, making it a must-visit destination for unforgettable nights out.

The Duke of Oz, opened in September 2009 by Marco and Paul, is an Australian-themed sports pub in Leiden, offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Known as the top sports bar in town, it boasts numerous screens so fans never miss a game—true to its motto, "If it’s on, it’s on." Whether you're there to catch your favorite team or simply enjoy a drink, you'll find friendly service and a diverse crowd. Designed to feel like a second home, The Duke invites you to relax, watch sports, and hear more about their story over a cold drink.

Located in the heart of Leiden, Poolcafé The Church offers a unique experience with its four pool tables, slot machines, large screen, dartboard, and cozy bar—all housed in a former Roman Catholic church. Reservations are encouraged, especially for larger groups and on weekends. Since July 1, 2008, a separate smoking area has been available to ensure a pleasant environment for all guests. Enjoy a fun and relaxed atmosphere while playing games and socializing in this distinctive venue!
