Restaurants

Beniras Bar & Kitchen is inspired by the town of Benirras in Ibiza, brings a warm, relaxed atmosphere to Leiden. The restaurant blends flavors from Mediterranean cuisines, including Greece, Spain, France, and Italy, offering a diverse culinary experience. Dishes are served in a shared dining style, encouraging guests to taste and share multiple options with their companions. Alongside its carefully crafted dishes, Beniras also boasts an extensive cocktail selection. With its focus on both taste and presentation, the restaurant combines delicious flavors with the laid-back ambiance of Ibiza, creating a unique dining experience in Leiden.

Since 1998, Restaurant Alexander has offered exceptional service and an unforgettable Asian dining experience. Renowned for its authentic flavors and warm atmosphere, it has built a loyal customer base. Our skilled chefs use fresh ingredients to create vibrant Asian dishes, prepared in an open kitchen where guests can watch the culinary magic unfold. Each dish is crafted with care, ensuring bold and delightful flavors. Whether you're a regular or a first-time guest, we guarantee a memorable meal that will leave you eager for more. Reserve your table today and experience the magic of Restaurant Alexander!

Yuki Sushi in Leiden is a beloved sushi restaurant known for its fresh, high-quality ingredients and authentic Japanese flavors. The menu features a wide variety of sushi rolls, sashimi, and traditional dishes, ensuring something for everyone. With skilled chefs dedicated to precision and care, each dish is crafted to perfection. Yuki Sushi’s modern yet cozy atmosphere makes it ideal for casual dining, special occasions, or takeout. The restaurant also offers vegetarian and gluten-free options, catering to diverse dietary needs. Popular with locals and visitors alike, Yuki Sushi promises an authentic and satisfying Japanese dining experience in Leiden.
